Expedition to Houston 2016

For a weekend in December, the Barbara Jordan Post Office in Houston, Texas will be turned into an immersive art installation and performance hall by Aphex Twin, Bjork, Squarepusher, John Carpenter, ODESZA, Butthole Surfers, Kaskade, Chet Faker, Travis Scott, Run The Jewels, Banks, Blood Orange, Little Dragon, The Jesus and Mary Chain and a couple dozen other acts. It’s Aphex Twin’s first US show in 8 years.

The show is called Day for Night

What's an Expedition

SF EMBASSY Missions to Iceland Airwaves and SXSW were 100 person week-long adventures with dozens of events and the logistical challenges to match. Expeditions are smaller in scale, shorter in duration, and we expect them to be easier to plan.
